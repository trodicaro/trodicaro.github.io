The art of asking questions

Or how to ask so you receive an answer

07/26/15

I have yet to post a question on StackOverflow or such sites. Partly because I am usually able to find an answer via a quick google search, but partly because my impostor syndrome won't let me: what if my question is not good enough? What if it's silly and laughable? Nah, I'm better off struggling on my own :D

Well if (or better yet, when) I post my first question on StackOverflow, I will make sure to keep Eric Steven Raymond's guide on How To Ask Questions The Smart Way handy.

A couple of the pieces of advice that we could all use:
  • Google your answer first (I guess I'm doing somting right...). Chances are there's a thread out there somewhere answering your question.
  • Choose your forum carefully: don't post a beginners question on an advanced forum.
  • Use titles with meaningful, to the point content
  • Make it easy to reply: create a reply-to header in your mail agent
