Hashes are pairs of elements. I like to think of them as children walking in pairs during a school trip.

Defining hashes:

A. 
my_info = {}
my_info[:name] = "Rodica"
my_info[:hometown] = "Braila"
my_info[:age] = 34



B.
my_info = {:name => "Rodica", :hometown => "Braila", :age => 34}
