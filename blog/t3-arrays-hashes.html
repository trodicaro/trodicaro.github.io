Arrays and Hashes

How to get a hang of both

06/08/15

1. Hashes are pairs of elements. I like to think of them as children walking in pairs during a school trip.

Initialize hashes:

a.	my_info = {}
	my_info[:name] = "Rodica"
	my_info[:hometown] = "Braila"
	my_info[:age] = 34
b.	my_info = {:name => "Rodica", :hometown => "Braila", :age => 34}

2. Arrays are like neatly lined up cubbies where first one is indexed with 0

Initialize arrays:

a.	class_roster = []
	class_roster[0] = "Jovanni"
	class_roster[1] = "Sebastian"
	class_roster[2] = "Keira"
b.	class_roster = ["Jovanni", "Sebastian", "Keira"]
